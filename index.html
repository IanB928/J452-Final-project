Why Choose Dayton

Affordable Living

Dayton is an affordable alternative to the high costs of living in nearby cities.

Dayton is positioned near several major employment areas with quick drive times.

No HOA allows you to keep those fees and dues in your pocket

Community Atmosphere

Dayton offers a friendly, small-town atmosphere with a strong sense of community.

Enjoy local events, farmers markets, and community gatherings that foster connections among residents.

Take in the history of Nevada and some of the first settlements to the Silver State.

Natural Beauty

Surrounded by stunning landscapes, Dayton provides ample opportunities for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and fishing.

Experience breathtaking views and a peaceful environment that enhances your quality of life.

Access to the Sierra Nevadas is a short drive and with little traffic.
